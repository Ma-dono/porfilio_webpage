About me ?

My name: Konan Gnissan Marie-Dominique Noellie. Born in Ivory Coast, more precisely in the city of Daloa. This is where I will do all my primary and my secondary school. With, initially the desire to become a doctor surgeon, my dreams have no continuation when at the Baccalaureate, I hardly obtain the grades sufficient to realize them.

I then spent, by force of circumstance, my first year of higher education in a school specializing in accounting: CPDEC (Centre de Preparation aux diplomas de l'Expertise Comptable). Unfortunately, I ended up not finding my happiness there and failed miserably in the first exams.

This is how my older sister, a computer scientist, enrolled me in GOMYCODE's Junior Web Developer training, during the holidays following the exams. And while I was not expecting it, I obviously found a passion for IT in general. I then continued with a BTS in computer science and a three-year engineering cycle. I also took care to add to my experience other GOMYCODE training such as Coding with python, Business technologies, and others.
